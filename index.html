Johnson County, IA Flood Risk Areas


Digital Scholarship & Publishing Studio, University of Iowa Libraries

This is a map of FEMA flood risk areas in Johnson County, Iowa. These risk classifications include 1-percent-annual-chance flood event, 0.2-percent-annual-chance flood event, and areas of minimal flood risk. Click on the map layers for more details. Flood data can be found at the BTAA Geoportal. Iowa county boundaries can be found at the Iowa Geodata web resource.
